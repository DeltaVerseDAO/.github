## What This Is

DeltaVerseDAO is the **governance arm** of the DeltaVerse — focused on how decentralized virtual worlds make collective decisions, distribute resources, and enforce rules through smart contracts. The collection spans voting systems, multisig wallets, airdrop tools, reputation contracts, and treasury management.
